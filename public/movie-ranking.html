The Best Movies According to Akshat

My top 3 movies of all-time.

INTERSTELLER

This is my favourite movie. The music given by Hans Zimmer, Direction of Christopher Nolan and an amazing star cast makes this movie beautiful.

THE GODFATHER

This classic gangster movie depicting the italian mafia families in USA is a masterpiece. The character of Don Vito Corleone is really strong and moving. This is Al Pacino's finest works.

AVENGERS: INFINITY WAR AND ENDGAME

Marvel delivers a cult movie for its fans, uniting all the heroes against a powerful enemy Thanos. Entertaining, emotional, world class CGI, this is a treat for the fans.
